Global Asset Monitoring

Asset tracking takes on whole new meaning when you expand the scope to include global operations. Time series and geo-location data can provide an enterprise with dramatic new insights into business processes, allow deep introspection of time and locality related metrics and push just in time operations to new levels.

Capture your global data where it gets generated and automatically replicate it to multiple data centers across the world for redundancy and the fastest possible local access. Datastax and Cassandra provide both the performance and capacity to capture volumes of enterprise data never imagined before.

DataStax monitors employee movements across the globe to better understand customer needs and geo-located workloads.
