Asset Tracking

The ability to manage something is frequently limited by the ability to understand where that something is in relation to others. A key mechanical part in location A is of little use when it is needed in location B. Important staff need to be located where they can interact with customers most effectively. The common issue is digesting information about dynamically changing assets and presenting this information in a way that can be useful in optimizing them.

This demonstration shows an example of acquiring location information and presenting it in a way that makes assets movement understandable and actionable.
